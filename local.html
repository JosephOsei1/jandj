LOCAL BEVERAGES

  • Sobolo(Hibiscus Tea)

    Sobolo is a refreshing and vibrant red drink made from dried hibiscus petals, ginger, and often sweetened with sugar. It's a common and beloved beverage enjoyed throughout Ghana.

  • Palm Wine

    Palm wine is a traditional alcoholic beverage obtained from the sap of palm trees. It can be enjoyed fresh or fermented and has a slightly sweet taste.

  • Asaana (Elewonyo)

    Asaana is a spicy and caramelized corn drink. It's made by soaking fresh crushed corn for about three days for fermentation and boiling for some few minutes and sweetening it with sugar or honey. Asaana is known for its zesty and warming flavor.

  • Brukina(Millet Drink)

    Brukina is a millet-based beverage that's creamy and slightly sweet. It's often flavored with spices like cinnamon and cloves and is a popular drink during festivals and gatherings.

  • Soya Milk

    Soya milk is a nutritious and protein-rich beverage made from soybeans. It's usually sweetened and flavored with vanilla or other spices.

  • fruit Juices

    Ghana boasts an abundance of tropical fruits, and you'll find a variety of fresh fruit juices available, including pineapple, mango, and orange juice.
